About

Started as a homework assignment for the Odin Project, the Music Archive has become a pet project for me to practice coding. I am hoping each Monthly Edition will help me visualize my progress in Html, CSS, & Java.

Everything here is my honest sardonic, sarcastic, and serious take on the music in my life. I hope you can discover some groovy tunes and cool cats while you are here. I am always taking recommendations and fresh takes, but remember this screen can't prevent you from catching these hands.

Why a Music Archive?

I enjoy listening to music a little too much and I often find myself wanting to remember what I enjoyed in the past. I find the old adage "you are what you eat" applies to my taste in artists, sounds, and genres. It's nice to reflect and recover gems from my past.

Is this a serious review of music? Absolutely not! More than anything else, the Music Archive is a blog/journaling hybrid for me to play the historian before I forget things.
